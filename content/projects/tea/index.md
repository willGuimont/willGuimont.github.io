Tea Shelf is a static, local-first PWA for saving Camellia Sinensis teas, caching product details, and tracking brew timers.

It lets you:

- Search Camellia Sinensis from the app and save teas with a browser bookmarklet.
- Cache descriptions, product information, flavour wheels, packages, and brewing methods locally.
- Track teas you own or like and sort your shelf by last brew, name, or date added.
- Run brew timers that automatically update when each tea was last brewed.
- Import and export local backups of your collection and brew history.
